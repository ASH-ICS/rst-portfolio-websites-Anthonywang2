Weekly Journal

Week 1
In the first week of the course, we learned the basic outline of the course and filled out a first day "Getting to know you" slide. We also watched a movie called Hidden Figures, and created a website.

Week 2
On the second week we learned about HTML and how to code. We also learned about binary and decimal and how to convert them and learned about logic gates. At the end of the week we had a quiz on binary and decimal.

Week 3
On the third week we started to learn about the different parts of a computer, and learned about different types of ports and buttons on a computer. We started to work on a poster about each computer part and software. Me and Evan Stewie chose to do Malware for our poster.

Week 4
On week four, we finished our computer part/software posters and me and Evan presented our malware poster. We also started working on CMU. In CMU, we learned to make and color shapes such as circles, rectangles and stars using python. On friday for the day of reconciliation we made an indigenous picture in CMU.

Week 5
On Monday we continued to work on our websites because we didn't work on them on Friday due to the reconciliation project. At the end of the week, Nokia came and gave a presentation.

Week 6
On week 6, we worked more on Tech Tuesday and the website Portfolio, as well as doing more CMU and then, we had a quiz on CMU at the end of the week. Scored a 9/10, so I'm happy with a 90%. We finished the week off with more work on the portfolio.

Week 7
After the CMU Quiz on Unit 1 last week, in week 7 we moved onto CMU Unit 2. We worked on another Tech Tuesday, this week me and Evan did our topic on AI. At the end of week 7 we worked more on our Portfolio website and then built pumpkins on Minecraft.

Week 8
On week 8, we started to learn more about artificial intelligence or AI. For the Tech Tuesday this week, we also did a topic about AI. There were some lessons about AI, and we learned about how AI can be rule based or data driven, and we had an example with a formula that can win every match in tic tac toe, which is an example of the rule based intelligence.

Week 9
On week 9, we started with CMU Monday working on unit 2, then moved into Tech Tuesday with this week's topic being Blockchains. We did another CMU Project, this time a project about Halloween with mouse events, and me and Evan made a pumpkin with glowing eyes on the mouse event. We learned more about AI the next 2 days working on Lesson 3 and 4 with classification and decision trees.

Week 10
On week 10, we started again with CMU Monday, did tech tuesday and did lesson 5 of AI. On Wednesday we did the final lesson of AI, on Thursday there was a remembrance day assembly.

Week 11
On week 11, we did CMU Monday, then did Tech Tuesday on the topic of cybersecurity, and started a project with AI on Rock Paper Scissors. The next two days we learned about Blockchains, and on Friday we had a quiz on CMU Unit 2.

Week 12
On week 12, we started with a new program called CodeHS, where we started to learn JavaScript and did some Scratch. We also moved our original websites from Replit onto GitHub.

Week 13
On week 13, we started with CMU Monday like usual, and from Tuesday to Thursday we started to learn and complete lessons on the subject Cybersecurity on CodeHS. On Friday we continued working on our Portfolio, but this time on the new GitHub website.

Week 14
On week 14, we worked on and finished some of the units in CodeHS including the Cybersecurity unit where we had to complete it with a PSA project. We had a little tetris game with the whole class and people from the government (I think) came over to demonstrate some new tech such as portable wifi. They also brought over Augmented Reality headsets to try out

Week 15
On week 15, I believe we were working more on CodeHS in further units as well as CMU, continuing to work on CMU Unit 3.
